About Us

Who We Are

Founded in 2016, Nova Chain Ventures was established to identify, develop, and support high-impact blockchain initiatives with real-world utility. The firm operates at the intersection of capital, research, and execution — combining strategic investment with hands-on venture building.

Over the years, Nova Chain has deployed over $790,000 into internal ventures, pilot programs, and strategic initiatives across fintech, decentralized infrastructure, digital identity, and emerging market solutions. Our approach emphasizes long-term value creation, disciplined capital allocation, and deep technical understanding.

We partner closely with builders, researchers, and ecosystem participants to transform early ideas into scalable platforms — providing more than capital, but strategic guidance, network access, and operational insight.

Fintech / Blockchain Payments

NovaPay

NovaPay is a blockchain-powered payment solution designed to enable fast, secure, and low-cost digital transactions. The platform focuses on simplifying cross-border payments and improving financial access for individuals and small businesses in emerging markets

Status: Active Development
Blockchain Infrastructure

ChainLogix

ChainLogix provides decentralized infrastructure tools that help businesses track, verify, and secure data across supply chains. By leveraging blockchain transparency and immutability, ChainLogix aims to reduce fraud and improve operational efficiency

Status: Pilot Project
Agric/Blockchain Solution

AgroChain

AgroChain applies blockchain technology to agriculture by improving traceability, data accuracy, and trust between farmers, distributors, and buyers. The project aims to support transparency and fair value exchange within the agricultural ecosystem

Status: Early Exploration
Web3 Identity

NovaID

NovaID is a decentralized identity solution that allows users to securely control and verify their digital identity without relying on centralized authorities. The project focuses on privacy, user ownership, and seamless integration with Web3 applications

Status: Active
Digital Payments

PayNest

PayNest is a digital payment platform focused on simplifying everyday transactions for individuals and small businesses. The company enables fast, secure, and affordable peer-to-peer and merchant payments, with a strong emphasis on usability and financial inclusion.
